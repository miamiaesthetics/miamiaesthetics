Our Courses

We cover an extensive array of courses, each specifically designed to address various aspects of medical aesthetics, ranging from facial treatments to body sculpting techniques. Below is a comprehensive overview of the courses we provide:

Botox and Botulinum Toxin Treatments

Our Botox and Botulinum Toxin course delves into the essential procedures of wrinkle relaxation and facial muscle smoothing. You’ll learn safe injection techniques, dosage management, and patient evaluation strategies. Whether you're new to aesthetics or looking to refine your skills, this course will equip you with the knowledge and practical expertise to confidently perform these treatments. We ensure a deep understanding of facial anatomy, safety protocols, and patient care.

Advanced Procedures: HIFU, Cryotherapy & Cryosurgery, Fat Reduction

In this course, we focus on non-invasive, advanced aesthetic procedures that are rapidly gaining popularity. High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) technology offers skin tightening and lifting without the need for surgery. Cryotherapy and Cryosurgery techniques are used for skin lesion removal and fat reduction treatments. Our fat reduction modules cover everything from basic to advanced body contouring, helping you provide your clients with effective, non-surgical fat loss options.

Facial Rejuvenation

This course is dedicated to one of the most sought-after treatments in aesthetics: Facial Rejuvenation. From basic skincare procedures to advanced facial sculpting techniques, you will master treatments that focus on restoring youthful vitality and radiance to your clients' skin.

Lasers & Lights (Basics and Advanced)

In our Lasers & Lights course, you will gain knowledge in the use of light-based technologies, which are widely used in aesthetic treatments for skin resurfacing, hair removal, pigmentation, and acne reduction. The course starts with the basics, covering laser physics, safety, and skin typing.

Microdermabrasion

Microdermabrasion is a cornerstone in skincare treatments, and our course covers the full scope of this popular procedure. From improving skin tone and texture to reducing the appearance of acne scars, this treatment is versatile and effective.

Skincare Treatments

Our skincare treatments course offers a deep dive into professional skincare techniques. You’ll cover everything from basic facials to advanced dermatological procedures that enhance skin health.

Weight Reduction

Specialize in non-surgical weight reduction techniques with our advanced course. From body contouring technologies to fat freezing treatments, we provide a detailed exploration of the tools and techniques required to help your clients achieve their desired body shape.
